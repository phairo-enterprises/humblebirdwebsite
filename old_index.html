About Humble Bird

Welcome to Humble Bird, where the art of Nashville Hot Chicken meets the heart and soul of our founder, Chef Luis Silva.

Chef Luis Silva

Chef Luis Silva, also known as Big Lu, is the mastermind behind the famous Luis Style Melt at Howlin' Ray's. Luis worked closely with Johnny Ray Zone and the crew in the fires of their Chinatown kitchen, earning massive respect and a strong bond forged through their shared passion for hot chicken. The plaza became famous for its 4-hour lines and the love that overflowed throughout Chinatown.

Bringing his skills and dedication to his own spot, Luis continues to create culinary magic at Humble Bird, offering an unforgettable spicy flavor explosion.
